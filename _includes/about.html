About GreenPipe Ltd


GreenPipe is an IT company that specialised in dataflow. We are Apache NiFi specialists and typically employ NiFi and its complementary applications to meet our customers needs.

We offer the full range of dataflow support including building infrastructure, writing bespoke code, defining/creating the flow, and remote or local support.

In addition we offer training to upskill customer staff with the knowledge required for long term support.
